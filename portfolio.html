Our Product

Vitawise Simulator

A "flight-simulator" type environment for surgeons that allows you to work on the 3D renderings of the body(organs) of a particular patient that are constructed from various scans input into the system and patient medical history. This will allow them to be familiar with the various complications that may arise in the surgical procedure.

Vitawise Alfred Butler

The future of long-distance surgery, Alfred is an AI agent that tracks your trial procedures. It runs this video feed through an AI engine to analyse the procedure. It uses this analysis to understand the surgical procedure and be present in the surgical theatre as a guide for the surgeon, alerting relevant people in case of any deviation from the original procedure.

Vitawise Hub

Our mixed reality environment allows you to collaborate by bringing in surgeons from anywhere around the world to supervise your trial/real surgeries, provide real-time second opinions, suggest alternative techniques for handling complications and much more. This extends the surgical theatre to beyond the premises of your hospital/medical institution.

Seamless Integration

We integrate with most Hospital Management Systems in order to pull patient records and push surgery reports seamlessly into your database.

Simple Solutions to Complex Problems

Healthcare is a complex industry with many stakeholders all the way from patients to the government. Therefore, any company building a solution in this space must be aware of the stakeholders and their interests. Hence, we have made a modest attempting at answering some of the questions most frequently posed to us by various stakeholders.

Can I deploy Vitawise on my hospital premises? - +

Yes, at Vitawise we provide you with a setup that allows you to set it up on your premises however online connectivity is still required for it to work perfectly.

I am a surgeon, where can I learn how to use vitawise? - +

If your hospital is already a customer of Vitawise, then you can ask them to request us for an on-premises product demo via email.

How do you handle sensitive healthcare data without compromising patient privacy? - +

We store data in the closest geography to each patient in an encrypted format. In case any breach is detected in our system we perform quick offline backups in those geographies and delete anything present on the cloud. Then we return all the data back online once the breach has been identified and closed. Also, all our systems comply with the government regulations in those regions.
